So you're thinking about being an AD Carry main?

Think no more! Here we'll give you all the reasons why you would ever consider picking AD Carry as you main role!

Attack Damage Carries (ADCs) have been around since the meta of League of legend was created. A characteristic of ADC is they tend to long range champions dealing a ton of damage. Throughout the years, there were many great changes to League of legends. However, ADC had stayed relatively the same. This does seem like it will change in the near future! So what better time then now to pick up the ADC role. There will always be a need for an ADC! The outlook looks great!
